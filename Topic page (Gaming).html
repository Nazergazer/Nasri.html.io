Brief Information About Gaming

What is gaming?

Gaming content on YouTube includes videos and live streams about games such as gameplay videos, speed runs, and different game reviews. It's one of the most watched categories on YouTube and even has its own section.

Why is it so popular?

Gaming content is popular on YouTube because it attracts many viewers who like to watch other people play games and also like to interact with the gaming community, such as learning tips from gamers.

The Different Types of Gaming Videos on YouTube

Gameplay Videos

The player shows what it's like to play the game, often with commentary from the gamer.

Walkthroughs

The player helps viewers by showing how to beat parts of a game.

Let’s Plays

The gamer plays through a game for fun and talks while playing.

Multiplayer Matches

The gamer play online games with or against others and share the fun moments.

Montages

Short clips of cool, exciting, or funny game moments are edited together.

Machinimas

Gamers use games to make short films or stories using the game graphics.

Live Streams

Gamers play live and interact with viewers while playing.

Reviews

The gamer gives their opinion on whether a game is good or bad.

Challenge Videos

Players try to complete different random challenges in a game.

Speedruns

Players try to finish games as fast as they can, aiming to break records.

Fan Videos

Videos made by fans, like game music videos or meme edits from the videos they sourced.

Tutorials

The gamers teach the viewers how to do something tricky in a game.

News Videos

Updates and news about new games or gaming events often are more unprofessional compared to actual news videos.

Video Essays

Gamers explore deeper ideas or stories behind games.
